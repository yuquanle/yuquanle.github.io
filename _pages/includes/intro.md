# 😊 About me
Hello! I am a Lecturer at Hunan Normal University. I received my Ph.D. degree from Hunan University in June 2025, supervised by Prof. [Chubo Liu](https://csee.hnu.edu.cn/people/liuchubo) and Prof. [Kenli Li](https://csee.hnu.edu.cn/people/likenli). From September 2023 to September 2024, I was a visiting Ph.D. student in the [Speech and Language Lab](https://aseschng.github.io/speechLab_intro.html) at Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Prof. [Eng Siong Chng](https://aseschng.github.io/default.html).

Before this, I completed my master’s degree at Hunan University under the supervision of Prof. [Zhe Quan](https://csee.hnu.edu.cn/people/quanzhe). After my master’s, I worked as an NLP researcher at JD AI Lab in Beijing, mentored by Dr. [Meng Chen](https://chenmengdx.github.io/) and Prof. [Xiaodong He](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=W5WbqgoAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN).

My current research interests mainly include：
-	Natural Language Processing
-	Large Language Models
-	Legal AI
-	Multimodal Learning

I enjoy sharing AI knowledge on the Weixin Official Account '[机器智元](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/d5ZurfqOXwIK-2WznGUOUw)' and my personal blog on '[CSDN](https://ainlp.blog.csdn.net/?type=blog)'.
